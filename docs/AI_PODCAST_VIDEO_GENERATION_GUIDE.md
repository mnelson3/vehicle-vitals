## Option 1: NotebookLM (Recommended for Podcast)

**What:** Google's AI tool that generates podcasts from documents  
**Cost:** Free (in beta)  
**Time to podcast:** 5–10 minutes  
**Quality:** Professional, natural-sounding dialogue

### How to Generate Podcast with NotebookLM

1. **Go to:** https://notebooklm.google.com

2. **Create a new notebook** and upload the podcast script:
   - Copy content from `/docs/PROMOTIONAL_PODCAST_SCRIPT.md`
   - Paste into NotebookLM

3. **Generate Audio Overviews:**
   - Click "Audio Overview" button
   - Select "Podcast" format
   - Choose two hosts (male/female recommended for variety)
   - Click "Generate"
   - Wait 2–3 minutes for audio generation

4. **Customize Output:**
   - Download MP3 file
   - Edit with Audacity if needed (add music, adjust volume)

5. **Distribute:**
   - Upload to Spotify, Apple Podcasts via Anchor/Buzzsprout
   - Share on YouTube as video with static background

**Pros:**

- ✅ Completely free
- ✅ Natural-sounding voices
- ✅ Fast (5 min to podcast)
- ✅ Two-person dialogue format
- ✅ Professional quality

**Cons:**

- Limited customization
- May not follow exact script (creates conversational version)

## Option 2: Descript (for Video with AI Voiceover)

**What:** AI-powered video creation tool with synthetic voices  
**Cost:** Free tier available ($24/month for pro)  
**Time to video:** 15–30 minutes  
**Quality:** Professional product demo video

### How to Generate Video with Descript

1. **Go to:** https://www.descript.com

2. **Create a new project** → "Screen Recording"

3. **Upload video footage or create from screenshots:**
   - Use actual screenshots from `/docs/screenshots/` (web + iOS)
   - Create slide deck (screenshots timed to audio)

4. **Add AI voiceover:**
   - Paste script from `PROMOTIONAL_VIDEO_SCRIPT.md`
   - Choose voice (male/female, accent, tone)
   - Descript auto-syncs audio to video
   - Adjust timing as needed

5. **Add captions:**
   - Click "Auto-caption"
   - Descript generates captions automatically
   - Edit for accuracy

6. **Export:**
   - Download as MP4 (1080p or 4K)
   - Ready for YouTube, TikTok, social media

**Pros:**

- ✅ AI voiceover (natural-sounding)
- ✅ Auto-captioning
- ✅ Easy screenshot-to-video workflow
- ✅ Professional output
- ✅ Free tier available

**Cons:**

- Requires screenshot prep
- May need manual timing adjustments

## Option 3: ElevenLabs (for Premium Voiceover)

**What:** AI voiceover platform with premium synthetic voices  
**Cost:** Free tier: 10K characters/month | Paid: $11+/month  
**Time to voiceover:** 2–5 minutes  
**Quality:** Extremely professional, natural-sounding

### How to Generate Voiceover with ElevenLabs

1. **Go to:** https://elevenlabs.io

2. **Sign up for free account**

3. **Create new project → "Text to Speech"**

4. **Paste your script:**
   - Use `/docs/PROMOTIONAL_PODCAST_SCRIPT.md` for podcast
   - Use `/docs/PROMOTIONAL_VIDEO_SCRIPT.md` for video voiceover

5. **Choose voice:**
   - Browse 100+ AI voices
   - Recommended: "Thomas" (professional), "Bella" (warm)
   - Adjust speed, pitch, emphasis

6. **Generate & Download:**
   - Click "Generate"
   - Wait 1–2 minutes
   - Download MP3

7. **Integrate with video:**
   - Import MP3 into video editor (DaVinci Resolve, CapCut, Adobe Premiere)
   - Sync with screenshot timeline
   - Add captions & music

**Pros:**

- ✅ Extremely natural-sounding
- ✅ 100+ voice options
- ✅ Free tier available
- ✅ Great for professional voiceovers

**Cons:**

- Limited free tier
- Requires separate video editing tool

## Option 4: Synthesia (for AI Avatar Video)

**What:** AI video creation with talking avatars  
**Cost:** Free tier ($25+/month for paid)  
**Time to video:** 20–40 minutes  
**Quality:** Professional video with AI presenter

### How to Generate Video with Synthesia

1. **Go to:** https://www.synthesia.io

2. **Create new video** → "From script"

3. **Paste script:**
   - Use full `PROMOTIONAL_VIDEO_SCRIPT.md`
   - Or use 60-second version

4. **Choose avatar:**
   - Select AI presenter (realistic avatar)
   - Choose language/accent
   - Customize appearance

5. **Customize video:**
   - Add background (use screenshots as backdrop)
   - Add text overlays (captions from script)
   - Add music/sound effects
   - Choose video resolution

6. **Generate & Download:**
   - Process takes 3–5 minutes
   - Download MP4
   - Ready to publish

**Pros:**

- ✅ Professional video with AI presenter
- ✅ Customizable backgrounds/overlays
- ✅ No filming required
- ✅ Multi-language support

**Cons:**

- Less free features
- AI avatar may feel less authentic than voiceover

## Step-by-Step: Create Podcast with NotebookLM

### Step 1: Prepare Script

```
Copy this content from /docs/PROMOTIONAL_PODCAST_SCRIPT.md:
[Lines 1–50, the main podcast content without production notes]
```

### Step 2: Go to NotebookLM

- Navigate to: https://notebooklm.google.com
- Click "+ New Notebook"

### Step 3: Upload Content

- Click "Upload" or paste text
- Paste the podcast script content
- Wait for processing (1–2 minutes)

### Step 4: Generate Podcast

- Look for "Audio Overview" button
- Select "Podcast" format
- Choose 2 hosts (NotebookLM will assign voices)
- Click "Generate"
- Wait 3–5 minutes

### Step 5: Download

- Click "Download" (MP3 format)
- Save to: `/docs/generated/podcast.mp3`

### Step 6: Enhance (Optional)

- Add intro/outro music in Audacity
- Add sound effects where appropriate
- Normalize audio levels
- Export as MP3

### Step 7: Distribute

- Upload to Anchor: https://anchor.fm
- Automatically distributes to Spotify, Apple Podcasts, etc.
- Takes 24–48 hours to appear

## Step-by-Step: Create Video with Descript

### Step 1: Prepare Materials

- Have screenshots ready from `/docs/screenshots/`
- Have script ready from `/docs/PROMOTIONAL_VIDEO_SCRIPT.md`

### Step 2: Go to Descript

- Navigate to: https://www.descript.com
- Sign up or log in
- Click "+ New project"

### Step 3: Create Video Timeline

- Option A: Upload existing video (if you have one)
- Option B: Create from scratch
  - Click "Paste text"
  - Paste voiceover script
  - Descript generates timeline

### Step 4: Add Voiceover

- Paste script: `PROMOTIONAL_VIDEO_SCRIPT.md` voiceover section
- Select AI voice (or use ElevenLabs link + import)
- Descript auto-generates audio and syncs

### Step 5: Add Screenshots as Visuals

- Click "Edit" in timeline
- Upload screenshots (drag/drop)
- Position screenshots to match voiceover sections
- Adjust timing (drag to sync with audio)

### Step 6: Auto-Caption

- Click "Captions" button
- Select "Auto-caption"
- Descript generates captions in real-time
- Edit captions for accuracy

### Step 7: Add Music & Effects (Optional)

- Click "Add" → "Music"
- Choose royalty-free track (Descript has library)
- Adjust volume so voiceover is primary
- Add transitions between scenes

### Step 8: Export

- Click "Export"
- Select resolution: 1080p or 4K
- Choose format: MP4
- Wait for rendering (2–5 min depending on length)

### Step 9: Publish

- YouTube: https://youtube.com/upload
- TikTok: https://www.tiktok.com/upload
- Instagram Reels: In app
- LinkedIn: In app

## Quality Checklist

Before publishing, verify:

### Podcast

- [ ] Audio is clear and audible
- [ ] No background noise/hum
- [ ] Volume levels are consistent
- [ ] Intro/outro music included
- [ ] Show notes written with CTA
- [ ] Upload to Anchor or Buzzsprout
- [ ] Verify distribution (Spotify, Apple)

### Video

- [ ] Resolution is 1080p minimum
- [ ] Captions are accurate and readable
- [ ] Text overlays are large enough
- [ ] Music volume doesn't overpower voiceover
- [ ] Transitions are smooth
- [ ] Thumbnail designed (YouTube)
- [ ] Title/description optimized for SEO
- [ ] Video is under platform time limits

## Troubleshooting

### NotebookLM Issue: "Audio generation failed"

- **Solution:** Try again or shorten the script
- **Alternative:** Use ElevenLabs instead

### Descript Issue: "Audio/video sync is off"

- **Solution:** Manually adjust timing by dragging scenes in timeline
- **Alternative:** Re-record with more deliberate pacing

### ElevenLabs Issue: "Character limit exceeded"

- **Solution:** Upgrade to paid tier OR split script into sections
- **Alternative:** Use free tier for shorter clips

### Video export is too slow

- **Solution:** Lower resolution (1080p instead of 4K) or shorten video
- **Alternative:** Use simpler video editor like CapCut
