# How to Generate Podcast with NotebookLM

## Step 1: Go to NotebookLM

1. Navigate to: https://notebooklm.google.com
2. Sign in with your Google account (free)

## Step 2: Create Notebook

1. Click "+New Notebook"
2. Name it: "Vehicle Vitals Podcast"

## Step 3: Upload Script

1. Click "Upload" or "Paste text"
2. Copy the content from: generated/podcast/PODCAST_CLEAN.txt
3. Paste into NotebookLM
4. Wait for processing (1-2 minutes)

## Step 4: Generate Audio Overview

1. Look for the "Audio Overview" button
2. Select "Podcast" format
3. Choose 2 hosts (NotebookLM assigns voices automatically)
4. Click "Generate"
5. Wait 3-5 minutes

## Step 5: Download

1. Click the download icon
2. Select MP3 format
3. Save as: generated/podcast/podcast_generated.mp3

## Step 6: (Optional) Enhance in Audacity

1. Open generated/podcast/podcast_generated.mp3 in Audacity (free)
2. Add intro/outro music if desired
3. Normalize audio levels (Effect > Normalize)
4. Export as MP3

## Done!

Your podcast is ready. Next:

- Upload to Anchor (anchor.fm) for automatic distribution to Spotify, Apple Podcasts
- Or upload to YouTube as a video with static background

Expected time: 15-20 minutes total
