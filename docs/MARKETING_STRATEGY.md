# Vehicle Vitals — Marketing Strategy & Launch Toolkit

**Document Type:** Marketing Strategy & Execution Guide  
**Last Updated:** May 8, 2026  
**Intended Audience:** Marketing team, content creators, executives

---

## Overview

This document outlines a comprehensive marketing strategy for Vehicle Vitals, leveraging the promotional assets created (podcast script, video script, user guide with screenshots, and feature documentation).

**Goal:** Establish Vehicle Vitals as the go-to vehicle management platform for individual owners and fleet managers through educational, benefit-driven content.

---

## Core Messaging

### Value Proposition

**Vehicle Vitals is the operating system for your vehicles — giving you complete visibility into costs, maintenance schedules, and services.**

### Key Benefits

1. **Financial Clarity** — Know your true cost of ownership
2. **Preventive Maintenance** — Catch issues before they become expensive problems
3. **Organization** — Everything in one secure place
4. **Reduced Stress** — Never wonder if you're forgetting something
5. **Time Savings** — Eliminate manual record-keeping
6. **Better Resale Value** — Documented maintenance history

### Target Segments

| Segment                 | Pain Point                                    | Benefit                           | Messaging                                      |
| ----------------------- | --------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- |
| **Individual Owners**   | Don't know true costs; scattered records      | Financial clarity + peace of mind | "Know what you're spending on your car"        |
| **Fleet Managers**      | Maintenance coordination chaos; uptime issues | Efficiency + predictability       | "Reduce breakdowns. Eliminate admin overhead." |
| **Small Business**      | Hidden operational costs; repair surprises    | Budget predictability             | "Smarter vehicle economics."                   |
| **Tech-Forward Buyers** | Tired of manual processes                     | Modern, cloud-based solution      | "Vehicle management, finally modernized."      |

## Campaign Timeline

### Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1–4)

- ✅ Finalize video scripts and podcast script
- ✅ Produce 3:20 video + 60-second clips
- ✅ Record podcast episode
- ✅ Create YouTube channel + post first videos
- ✅ Set up TikTok / Instagram accounts
- ✅ Launch blog with 3–5 cornerstone articles

**Metrics Goal:** 500 YouTube subscribers, 50 TikTok followers, 1,000 email list

### Phase 2: Acceleration (Weeks 5–12)

- Launch paid ads (Google + Facebook)
- Publish 2 blog posts weekly
- Post daily on TikTok, 3–4x weekly on Instagram/YouTube
- Send email campaigns 2x weekly
- Secure 1–2 podcast guest appearances
- Partner with 1–2 automotive channels for cross-promotion

**Metrics Goal:** 2,000 YouTube subscribers, 10,000 TikTok followers, 5,000 email list, 200+ trial signups

### Phase 3: Scale (Weeks 13+)

- Expand paid ads based on ROI
- Publish case studies / customer testimonials
- Increase content frequency based on performance
- Explore brand partnership opportunities
- Target 1,000+ MRR from paid channels

**Metrics Goal:** 10,000 YouTube subscribers, 50,000 TikTok followers, 15,000 email list, 1,000+ monthly trial signups

## Resource Requirements

### Creative / Production

- **Video Production:** 40–60 hours (scripting, recording, editing, optimization)
- **Podcast Production:** 20–30 hours (recording, editing, distribution)
- **Content Writing:** 80–120 hours (blog posts, email campaigns, social copy)
- **Design:** 20–30 hours (thumbnails, graphics, templates)

### Tools & Software

- **Video Editing:** Adobe Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro, or DaVinci Resolve
- **Podcast Hosting:** Anchor, Buzzsprout, or Podbean
- **Email Marketing:** ConvertKit, Mailchimp, or ActiveCampaign
- **Social Media Management:** Buffer, Hootsuite, or Later
- **Analytics:** Google Analytics, YouTube Analytics, TikTok Analytics
- **Ad Platforms:** Google Ads, Facebook Ads Manager

**Estimated Monthly Cost:** $200–500 (tools + software)

### Team

- **Minimum:** 1 part-time marketing person (20 hours/week)
- **Recommended:** 1 full-time marketer + 1 part-time videographer/designer
- **Ideal:** 1 marketing director + 1 content creator + 1 video producer
